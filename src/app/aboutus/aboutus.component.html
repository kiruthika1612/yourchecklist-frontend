ABOUT US

Who are we?

We are a team of 3 people who decided to develop a site where you can get all essential items that are needed for travel. We are students at confederation college enrolled in Information for Communication technology for small business. We are one among millions of people who likes to travel and explore places.

Our Motivation

Our goal is to be able to bring together all essential items that would be needed for travel, which would make their travel a comfortable one. Our goal is to make our customers happy which in turn would make us happy.

Our goal is to increase the customer satisfaction through our service.
